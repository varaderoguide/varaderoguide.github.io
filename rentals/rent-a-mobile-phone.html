PUBLIC PHONES:

The number of public phones has increased in Cuba, from them you can make international calls with no need of international operator. Phone cards are $10.00, $20.00 and $50.00 cuc and you can buy them at hotels, stores and ETECSA offices (Cuban telephone company).


MOBILE PHONES:

Roamming contracts applies for almost all cellphone companies. If your phone operates under GSM 900 MHz (European standars) or TDMA 800 MHz (American standar) is very probable that you can use your own phone in Cuba or in some cases you'll need to activate your phone. More info at www.etecsa.cu


You can also rent a local mobile phone and save on roaming charges and enjoy the freedom of always to be in touch.


INTERNET:

You can access internet by buying a card and log in through any ETECSA's wi-fi hotspots in your hotel or on the streets, usually on public parks.

An internet access hour costs $1.00 cuc.
