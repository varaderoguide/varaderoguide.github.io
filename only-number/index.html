Customer service

"Only Number"

"Only Number" as indicated in the name is no more than all our services globalized in a single phone number.

It's an important and convenient tool that will get you in touch with our 24/7 Customer Service, designed for both EMERGENCY and buying SERVICES situations.

                        (53) 58414334

Emergency:

For "life-threating" situations involving accidents (car, domestic accidents) or robbery we strongly recommend you make the firts call to the Cuban Emergency Services, who have the resources to get to your location in the least time possible and put you to safe, usually there's an English spoken person at end of the line to guide you through.

Police 106

Ambulance 45662306

After you're safe please let us know you were involved in a mayor situation and we'll provide care and legal advise.

For non "life-threating" situations like if your lost or need assistance please call "Only Number" provided and we'll sort you out.
